I'm currently pursuing my MBA at XIMB after completing my undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering from Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering, Pune.

Born and raised in Amravati, Maharashtra, I've always been someone who enjoys learning by doing. Whether it was speaking on stage, participating in quizzes, leading student committees, or working on technical projects, I loved experiences that challenged me to think, adapt, and collaborate.

What Keeps Me Curious 🚀

My engineering journey gave me a strong analytical foundation, but it was real-world exposure that sparked my interest in management.

During my internship at the India Meteorological Department (IMD), Ministry of Earth Sciences, I worked alongside researchers and interns and observed how information, systems, and processes support decision-making at scale.

This continued through my final-year project sponsored by IMD, where my team studied existing weather-data systems and proposed improvements for future implementation. The experience strengthened my curiosity about how decisions are made, priorities are set, and organizations operate effectively.

And now, I'm particularly interested in the intersection of technology, management, operations, and people.
